Price is noise — a reflection of emotion, speculation, and cycles. But underneath that noise lies something far more disruptive: a system where control is returned to the individual. No bank can freeze it, no government can print more of it, and no intermediary can deny access.


The ones who truly understand crypto are not the ones watching charts all day — they are the ones who understand what it means to own something that cannot be taken away. YEs
Security remains one of the most important aspects of crypto participation. Phishing attacks, malicious smart contracts, and social engineering are common threats. Users must learn to verify sources, understand permissions, and avoid interacting with unknown or suspicious platforms. Unlike traditional systems, there is often no recourse once a mistake is made.

Education is one of the biggest barriers to entry.
